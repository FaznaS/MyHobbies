Let's look into other hobbies

Reading

When it comes to reading it doesn't have to be story books. It could be magazines, newspapers, science books, English books or any other. Reading helps you in increasing your knowledge on the related subject. It also helps to relax yourself and is a good hobby for the one's who wants to engage themselves alone in their spare time.

Gardening

Gardening is very good hobby. People engaged in gardening find it very relaxing. They love to see the flowers bloom. They say that when the flowers bloom bees and butterflies go from flower to flower and the scene makes them feel carm and everything looks charming. Everyday the plants are watered and it gives them a pleasure.

Collecting Stamps

There are many people who love to collect stamps. Some even collect stamps from other countries with the help of their relatives and friends. You can collect stamps and arrange them in a book with the details on how and from where you had got them. According to the stamp lovers every stamp has a story to tell of distant lands and strange people. Looking through the stamps you can follow the history of the nation.
