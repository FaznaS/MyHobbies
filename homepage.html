What is a hobby?

A hobby is an activity done by an individual in his/her free time. There are many hobbies that a person could have. Some of them are collecting stamps, collecting coins, drawing, reading, etc..

What are the types of hobbies?

You cannot specifically categorize hobbies into types because they belong to many categories. However, we can distinguish some types of hobbies.

Type Meaning
Social Hobby These are hobbies that you do with other people
Solo Hobby These are hobbies that you do when you want to be alone
Physical Hobby These are hobbies that help you to be physically active

Why do you need a hobby?

Hobbies play an important role in our lives. They are not merely activities done in your free time but at times the reason for the changes happening in your life. Hobbies like listening to music, dancing, singing and handcrafting are good to enhance your creative skills. Moreover, hobbies like cycling, skateboarding, gardening, and swimming help you to become physically fit. Nevertheless, there are other hobbies like coding, making diys, and cooking that may help you in becoming financially stable in the future. Apart from that hobbies also help you to forget your worries and inspire you. Therefore having a hobby is very important.

How to find your hobbies?

Finding a hobby is not a complicated process. Here are some steps you should follow to find your hobbies:

  • Decide whether you want to spend your free time alone or with a group
  • Find what motivates you more
  • Try to figure out why you are doing it
    • Is it to relax?
    • Is it for excitement?
    • Is it for popularity?

REMEMBER: Do not be afraid to change your hobby at any moment. Moreover, it is not necessary to stick to one hobby at a time. After all, it's a matter of achieving our goals.
