Introduction

I listen to music in my phone with earphones or with a bluetooth speaker. Therefore I can listen to them any time I want and whenever I feel to. I often listen to music after my studies, when I'm bored and also when I'm happy too. Sometimes I listen to songs when while travelling as well. I put the playlist in the shuffle mode as it is interesting wait until the next song plays to know what it is.

Music is always diverse because there are songs created in different parts of the world by a number of singers. There are many genres of songs like pop, classic and rock sung by many singers. I mostly like to liisten to pop music but when I'm in a bad mood I prefer calm music. At times I listen to instrumental music as well during a long break after my studies. I have no favorite singers, I listen to songs of all singers what matters to me is the content of the song and the beats.

Listening to music is very good and effective to me. It helps me to relax and cheer up my mood after a exhausting time period. Since it does not take much of time I consider this hobby of mine as my all time favourite.

I have a list of favorite songs. My most favourite favourite songs are "Believer by Imagine dragons", "Perfect by Ed Shereen" and "Try Everything by Shakira". The song "Try Everything" is a song which I have started to listen recently and it is also from the movie "Zootopia".
